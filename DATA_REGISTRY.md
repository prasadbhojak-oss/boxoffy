## DATA SOURCE HIERARCHY

When numbers conflict between sources, use this order:
1. **Box Office India (BOI)** — trade, most conservative, most reliable for net
2. **Koimoi closing reports** — good for final confirmed figures
3. **Sacnilk day-wise** — best for granular day/week breakdowns
4. **Bollywood Hungama** — cross-reference only
5. **Wikipedia** — good for production facts (producer, studio, music)
6. **Variety / South Asian Herald** — best for deal values (OTT, music rights)
7. **Trade analyst X posts** — for predictions only, clearly attributed
