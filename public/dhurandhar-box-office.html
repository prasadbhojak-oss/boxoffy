Boxoffy Verdict & Analysis
Boxoffy Verdict · All-Time Blockbuster
The film that reset every ceiling Indian cinema had set for itself.
Dhurandhar did not just break records — it broke the framework within which records were counted. ₹895 Cr India net on an estimated ₹250 Cr budget. 12 consecutive weeks in the Top 10. The highest-grossing Hindi film of all time — surpassing Jawan (₹640 Cr net), Animal (₹556 Cr), Stree 2 (₹621 Cr), Chhaava (₹550 Cr). It ran for 59 days on BookMyShow's trending list. It trended on Netflix for 6 weeks post-theatrical. Trade analyst Rohit Jaiswal documented the run in real time: "₹1300+ CRORE WORLDWIDE. ₹1000+ CRORE INDIA. HISTORY MADE. Dhurandhar doesn't just break records — it obliterates them." Sumit Kadel: "Running strong even in its 8th weekend. Fuelled purely by audience madness and iron-solid word of mouth." Produced by Jyoti Deshpande, Aditya Dhar & Lokesh Dhar under Jio Studios & B62 Studios. Music by Shashwat Sachdev (Saregama). Ranveer Singh's spy thriller became, simply, the biggest Hindi film ever made.

Data: Box Office India, Sacnilk, Koimoi. WW total includes overseas gross converted at prevailing rates.
